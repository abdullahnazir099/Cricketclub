About APU Cricket Club

Club History

Established with a passion for cricket and a commitment to sportsmanship, the AUP Cricket Club has a rich history of nurturing talent, fostering camaraderie, and promoting the values of fair play and teamwork. Over the years, we have proudly represented our community in various leagues, tournaments, and friendly matches, leaving our mark on the pitch and in the hearts of cricket lovers.

Our values

At the core of our club are the values of respect, integrity, and a love for the game. We believe in more than just cricket; we believe in creating a family where players forge lifelong friendships, celebrate victories together, and support each other through every inning, both on and off the field.
